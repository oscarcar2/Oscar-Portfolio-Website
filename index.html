Oscar Halbe

I am a hobby Game Designer and qualified Computer Science AP Grad.
I work on games with fellow graduates at our 3 owner company: Stranded Ocean Games.
Beyond Game Design, i also program, do game directing and a little bit of art, story and marketing.
My passion is designing and analyzing mechanics.
I have ~4 years of experience in amateur game development across several projects viewable on this site.
Right now I work on the current Stranded Ocean project: FloraHive!
